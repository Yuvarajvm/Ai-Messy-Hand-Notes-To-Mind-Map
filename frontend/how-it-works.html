How It Works

Transform your messy notes into structured mindmaps in just four simple steps.

1

Upload Your Notes

Take a photo of your handwritten notes, classroom board, or upload scanned documents. Our system supports JPG, PNG, PDF, and HEIC formats. You can upload multiple files at once for batch processing.

2

AI-Powered OCR Extraction

Our Google Vision OCR engine analyzes your images and extracts text with exceptional accuracy. It handles messy handwriting, low-light photos, and even recognizes diagrams and mathematical equations.

3

Intelligent Processing

Advanced AI models analyze the extracted text to identify key concepts, relationships, and hierarchies. The system automatically organizes information into structured topics and subtopics.

4

Get Your Mindmap

Receive an interactive mindmap with color-coded branches, key topics highlighted, and a clean text summary. You can edit, reorganize, and export your mindmap in multiple formats including PNG, PDF, and JSON.
